Our Moving Process

From start to finish, we've got every detail covered

1. Initial Consultation and Quote

The first step is a thorough consultation to understand your needs and requirements. We provide a detailed quote based on the size of your move, the distance, and any additional services you may require. Our goal is transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

2. Packing and Preparation

Once you've booked, we begin the packing and preparation process. Our team will arrive at your home with all the necessary packing materials and equipment. We carefully pack your belongings, labeling each box for easy identification.

3. Moving Day

On moving day, our uniformed team arrives on time and ready to work. We load your belongings onto our moving trucks with care, ensuring everything is secure for the journey. Our drivers are experienced in navigating various routes across Waikato.

4. Unpacking and Settling In

Upon arrival at your new home, we unload your belongings and place them in the designated rooms. We also offer unpacking services to help you settle in quickly. Our team will reassemble any furniture and remove all packing materials.
