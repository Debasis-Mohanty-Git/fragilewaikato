Storage Tips & Best Practices

Packing Tips

  • Use sturdy boxes: Invest in high-quality boxes to protect your items
  • Label everything: Clearly label each box with its contents and destination room
  • Disassemble furniture: Take apart large pieces to save space and prevent damage
  • Wrap fragile items: Use bubble wrap or packing paper to protect delicate items

Choosing the Right Unit Size

  • Small (1×3): Ideal for boxes, small furniture, and seasonal items
  • Medium (3×3): Perfect for contents of a one-bedroom house
  • Large (3×6): Suitable for multi-bedroom house contents, including appliances

Storing Tips

  • Create an inventory: Keep a detailed list of all items for easy reference
  • Utilise vertical space: Stack boxes and use shelving to maximise space
  • Keep essentials accessible: Place frequently needed items near the front
  • Climate considerations: Store sensitive items in climate-controlled units
